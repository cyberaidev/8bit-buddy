# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Assume good intent while addressing the work rather than
the person. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruptive behaviour are not
accepted in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when needed to keep the
community safe and productive. Report conduct concerns privately to a repository maintainer through
GitHub. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
